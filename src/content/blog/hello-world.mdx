Welcome to my blog! This is the first post on my new personal site.

## Why I Started This Blog

After years of building companies, leading teams, and working on hard problems in machine learning and optimization, I've decided it's time to write more publicly. This blog is a place where I'll share my thoughts on technology, AI, entrepreneurship, and whatever else I find interesting.

## What to Expect

I plan to write about:

- **AI reliability and testing** — the challenges of making AI systems trustworthy in production, which is what we're building at [Distributional](https://www.distributional.com)
- **Bayesian optimization** — a topic near and dear to my heart from my PhD work, building [MOE](https://github.com/Yelp/MOE) at Yelp, and founding [SigOpt](https://sigopt.com)
- **Startup lessons** — things I've learned founding two companies, going through YC, raising from top VCs, and navigating an acquisition
- **Research to product** — turning academic research into real-world products and businesses
- **Technical deep-dives** — detailed explorations of interesting problems in ML, optimization, and software engineering

## How This Site Works

This site is built with [Astro](https://astro.build) and deployed to AWS (S3 + CloudFront). Blog posts are written in MDX — Markdown with support for embedded components — and committed directly to the git repo. When I push to main, GitHub Actions builds and deploys automatically.

The entire site is static, fast, and cheap to host. No databases, no server-side rendering, no unnecessary JavaScript.

> The best tool is the one you actually use. I wanted something simple enough that writing a post is just writing a file.

Stay tuned for more posts!
